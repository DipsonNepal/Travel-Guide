Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to travel to Nepal?

Most travelers need a visa. Tourist visas are available on arrival at Kathmandu airport and land borders.

What is the best time to visit Nepal?

Autumn (Sep-Nov) and Spring (Mar-May) are the best trekking and sightseeing seasons.

Is Nepal safe for travelers?

Yes, Nepal is generally safe for tourists. Always take normal precautions.

What currency is used in Nepal?

The Nepalese Rupee (NPR) is the local currency.
